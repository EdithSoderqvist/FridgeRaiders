GARLIC CHICKEN PARCELS

 

PREP:10min › COOK:30min › READY IN:40min
 
  INGREDIENTS
2 chicken breasts
320g sheet all butter puff pastry
150g cream cheese
2 cloves of garlic
80g bag spinach
200g mixed pack green beans and/or broccoli
1 tbsp olive oil
Salt and black pepper to taste


  1. HEAT UP OVEN
    Heat oven to 220C/200C Fan/gas 7.

  2. PREPARE THE CHICKEN
    Cut a slit halfway in each chicken breast, then put each one between two pieces of baking parchment and bash with a rolling pin to flatten slightly. 

  3. ADD GARLIC TO THE CHEESE
    Press the two cloves of garlic and add to the creme cheese. Boursin or garlic & herb cream cheese works as well.    

  4. MIX CHICKEN, CHEESE AND PASTRY AND BAKE
    Cut your sheet of pastry in half widthways and put both halves on a baking tray. Sit a chicken breast on top of each half, then cram the cavities with the creeme cheese (don’t worry if a bit of cheese oozes out). Season, then fold the edges of the pastry in to the centre and pinch shut. Flip each parcel over so the seal is on the bottom. Brush with a little oil, then bake for 30 mins until deep golden.    

  5. BOIL THE VEGGIES
    About 10 mins before your parcels are ready, steam or boil the greens until tender. Toss in the oil and season. Serve alongside your chicken parcels.
